Let's Make Dreams Come True!

For one weekend only, the After Hours cafe will be hosting the second annual Innovation Workshop, a family-friendly event dedicated to gathering creatives from across the local area!

What is the Innovation Workshop?

When Chloe Rose opened the After Hours Cafe back in 2024, she never imagined that her dream sanctuary would be so successful. Despite the booming night life and community it brought in, she also knew that some of the biggest dreamers had earlier bedtimes. Furthermore, she realized that dreams could be cultivated at any age and that innovation wasn’t just for adults, but for kids too. Thus, she decided to open up the Innovation Workshop, a block party centered around cultivating art and creative writing. Through each session, students of all ages can learn to exchange ideas, brainstorm, and share treats all afternoon until the street lights came on.
